2. Story without words

For this project we supposed to create a video without words and then to put the video on youtube.

We started from the idea that bullying is a big problem in society.

We tried to highlight scenes in different environments and keep the same characters.
Bullying is a growing problem in today's world and we hope this video is suggestive enough to tell the story of a young adult suffering from injustice everyday.


3. Treatment

For this project we supposed to make a treatment.

We started from the idea to show how wasy it is for tourists to rent a bike in Copenhagen .

We choose Alex to be the main character who just came to Denmark and wants to rent a bike in Copenhagen , and then our team decided that the secondary characters would be me and Eva which will help Alex to rent a bike.


4. Story board

For this project we supposed to create a story board with all the scenes and framing .

First scene is at the airport when Alex is coming to Copenhagen.

Second scene is when Alex is trying to rent a bike and he fails.

Scene three is when two girls approaching Alex.

Scene four is when Alex is asking for help.

Scene five is when he find a better option.

Scene six he is riding away with the bike.
